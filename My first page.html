1. Cookies

Personally, I enjoy baking matcha chocolate chip cookies, sugar cookies, peanut butter cookies, and salt chocolate chip shortbread.

I understand that the latter is not technically a cookie as no egg is used, but they are still delicious.

All of them are feel-good snacks and even better when shared with your loved ones (not with enemies in this case).

2. Dogs

Dogs, dogs dogs. Where to begin? We humans surely don't deserve them!

For maximum cuteness, I personally recommend the fluffiest dogs possible, such as Alaskan malamutes, samoyedes or Australian shepherd dogs:

In any case, any and all dogs are a godsend and you should choose your canine companion(s) as you like! They will 100% make you happy.

Even when they're being difficult, it is known that all dogs are the goodest boys and girls.

3. Bubble tea

When I refer to the time-old adage - "a bubble tea a day keeps the doctor away" - please note that only a bubble tea will work for this technique, not just any tea.

What is a bubble tea you ask? This site gives a good explanation.

In terms of flavour, I personally enjoy matcha bubble teas or fruity green tea bubble teas. For those who like milky bubble teas, I also highly recommend brown sugar milk bubble teas!
